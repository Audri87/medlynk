# SD-005 — Strategic Design Baseline

**Status**: Release v1.0

---

## Purpose

Establish the certified Strategic Design baseline of the Clinical Platform.

This document freezes the strategic architecture and identifies the reference artefacts governing subsequent Software Architecture and implementation.

No new strategic decisions are introduced.

---

## 1. Strategic Scope

The Strategic Design applies exclusively to the Clinical Platform, the core platform of the MedLink ecosystem.

Future platforms (Learning, Trust, Collaboration, etc.) are explicitly out of scope.

## 3. Certified Core Domain

The Clinical Platform is centred around two Core Bounded Contexts.

| Bounded Context | Mission |
|---|---|
| Clinical Work | Coordinate clinical work while preserving Clinical Responsibility. |
| Clinical Knowledge | Preserve validated clinical knowledge generated by clinical work. |

Ownership boundaries are frozen.

---

## 4. Certified Architectural Principles

The following principles are mandatory.

- Business ownership is unique.
- Each Domain Concept has a single owner.
- Bounded Contexts communicate through Domain Events.
- Business rules remain inside the owning Bounded Context.
- Clinical Continuity is an emergent property of the platform.
- Workspace is a composition layer.
- Business Widgets are presentation components.
- External integrations pass through an Anti-Corruption Layer.

---

## 5. Strategic Dependency Rules

The dependency rules defined in SD-004 are adopted as mandatory.

- Clinical Knowledge never modifies Clinical Work.
- Clinical Work never reads Clinical Knowledge directly.
- Workspace owns no business logic.
- Business Widgets own no business rules.
- Read Models are never sources of truth.
- External integrations occur only through the Anti-Corruption Layer.

---

## 6. Deferred Decisions

The following topics are intentionally deferred to Software Architecture.

- Symfony module structure
- CQRS implementation
- Event Bus
- Persistence strategy
- API Architecture
- Business Widget Framework
- Workspace Composition
- Security Architecture
- Deployment Architecture

These decisions shall not alter the certified Strategic Design.

---

## 7. Change Governance

Any modification affecting:

- Core Business Capabilities
- Core Bounded Contexts
- Ownership boundaries
- Business invariants
- Domain Events crossing Bounded Contexts

requires a new Strategic Design review.

Feature additions, widgets and supporting capabilities do not require reopening the Strategic Design unless they impact these elements.

---

## 8. Strategic Evolution Policy

The Clinical Platform is designed to evolve through:

- new Supporting Business Capabilities;
- new Features;
- new Business Widgets;
- new Workspace compositions.

The certified Core Domain shall remain stable unless future Domain Discovery demonstrates a structural business change.

---

## 9. Handover to Software Architecture

The Strategic Design is considered complete.

Software Architecture shall implement the certified strategic decisions without redefining business ownership or strategic boundaries.
