# SD-003 — Clinical Platform Bounded Context Discovery

**Status**: Release v1.0

---

## Purpose

Define the Bounded Contexts of the Clinical Platform by identifying ownership boundaries, business invariants and collaboration mechanisms.

Bounded Contexts emerge from business ownership rather than from technical decomposition.

---

## 1. Discovery Principles

A Bounded Context shall exist only if it satisfies all of the following conditions:

- owns a coherent business model;
- owns business invariants;
- owns its ubiquitous language;
- evolves independently;
- has a clear business owner.

Business Capabilities are starting points.

They do not automatically become Bounded Contexts.

## 7. Strategic Consequences

The Clinical Platform is organised around two Core Bounded Contexts:

- Clinical Work
- Clinical Knowledge

Supporting capabilities consume, compose or expose information owned by these Contexts.

Business ownership remains concentrated within the Core Domain.

**Clinical Continuity** is an emergent property of the Clinical Platform.

It is not owned by either Core Bounded Context.

It results from the correct collaboration between Clinical Work and Clinical Knowledge: Clinical Work ensures that Clinical Responsibility is always held and transferred through accepted Clinical Handovers; Clinical Knowledge ensures that validated Clinical Contributions remain available across successive Clinical Activities.

Clinical Continuity exists when both Contexts fulfil their respective responsibilities correctly.

No single Bounded Context may claim ownership of Clinical Continuity.

---

## 8. Open Decisions

The following architectural decisions are intentionally deferred to Software Architecture:

- Workspace composition
- Business Widget model
- Search implementation
- Anti-Corruption Layer implementation
- Event Bus implementation
- API strategy

These decisions do not affect the strategic ownership boundaries.

---

**Informational — Clinical Handover BC Promotion**

Clinical Handover is currently part of the Clinical Work Bounded Context. Future platform evolution may justify promoting Clinical Handover to an independent Bounded Context if its business model, invariants and lifecycle become sufficiently autonomous.

This is not a current architectural decision. It is recorded here to ensure the question is not rediscovered without context. Promotion shall only be considered after resolution of H-HO-001, H-G1 and H-A-006.
